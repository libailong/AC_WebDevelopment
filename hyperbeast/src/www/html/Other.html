ASSC

Anti Social Social Club (often "ASSC") is a streetwear brand founded by Neek Lurk in Los Angeles. He previously worked for Stussy. The brand has collaborated with A Bathing Ape, Dover Street Market, and Playboy.

Vlone

Following the success of ASAP Mob, fellow member Kamoni Chandler, also known as A$AP K, founded the streetwear label VLONE in 2011. Shelton, who had previously been the head of A$AP Rocky's merchandise during his tours, worked together with Chandler on the brand. After a falling out Chandler left the brand and Shelton took over.

Others

Clot

CLOT is a streetwear label and lifestyle brand founded by Edison Chen and Kevin Poon, aimed the bridge Eastern and Western culture. The brand is active in doing collaboration and crossover with international brands like Disney, Nike, and Coca-cola.

Others

Li-Ning

Li-Ning Company Limited is a Chinese company which makes athletic shoes and sporting goods. The company endorses a number of athletes and teams, both in China and around the world.
